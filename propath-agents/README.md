# ProPath Agency Agents 🏆

Specialist AI agents tailored for ProPath — elite soccer & fitness mentorship platform.

## How to Use

Just tell me to "activate" an agent before your request:
> "Activate Growth Hacker — review our pricing page"
> "Activate Brand Guardian — check this Instagram caption"
> "Activate UX Researcher — what's wrong with our onboarding?"

I'll load that agent's persona and give you a specialist-level response.
