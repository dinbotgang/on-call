# TikTok Algorithm & Anti-Shadowban Guide
*3AM Narrator — Max Reach + High CPM*

## ✅ What the Algorithm Rewards (in order of importance)

1. **Watch-through rate** — % of people who watch the full video. Our 21-35s length is ideal.
2. **Rewatch rate** — twist endings make people rewatch. Engineer every story with this in mind.
3. **Comments** — always end caption with a question to trigger this.
4. **Shares** — horror content gets shared to friends ("watch this alone at 3am")
5. **Follows** — strong brand identity (profile pic, consistent niche) drives follows
6. **Likes** — least important signal but still counts

## 📝 Caption Formula (use every time)

```
[Engagement question about the story] [1-2 emojis]

#scarystories #horrortok #3am #creepy #scarytiktok #spooky #horrorstories #fyp #foryou #3amstories
```

**Rules:**
- Question MUST relate to the story — makes comments authentic
- 10 hashtags max (TikTok penalises hashtag spam)
- Always include #fyp #foryou — FYP signal
- Always include #horrortok — strong niche community
- Always include #3am — brand building
- Never repeat the same caption twice

## 🚫 Shadowban Prevention — CRITICAL RULES

### Never Do:
- ❌ Post more than 2 videos/day on a new account (first 30 days: max 1/day)
- ❌ Use banned hashtags (avoid: #fypage, #foryoupage — ironically banned on some accounts)
- ❌ Copy/paste same caption repeatedly
- ❌ Use copyrighted music (we use Kokoro TTS + Minecraft audio — safe)
- ❌ Post from a VPN (TikTok flags IP changes)
- ❌ Delete and repost videos (kills reach permanently)
- ❌ Use other platform watermarks (no TikTok logo from downloads)
- ❌ Post sexually suggestive, violent gore, or misinformation content
- ❌ Follow/unfollow in bulk

### Always Do:
- ✅ Post from same IP (VPS static IP — consistent)
- ✅ Complete profile before first post (bio, pfp, name — done)
- ✅ Original audio only
- ✅ Engage with comments within first hour of posting
- ✅ Consistent posting schedule (same time each day)

**Horror content exception:** Late night (10 PM–12 AM) can also work well — spooky content + actual dark environment = higher engagement. Test both.

## 💰 High CPM Strategy

CPM is driven by advertiser demand for YOUR audience. To maximise:

1. **Target US/UK/CA/AU audience** — highest CPM markets
   - Post in English, tag locations implicitly (don't use VPN that changes geo)

2. **18-34 demographic** — most valuable to advertisers
   - Our horror/thriller content naturally attracts this group

3. **Avoid CPM-killing content:**
   - No politics, religion, or controversy
   - No medical/health claims
   - No profanity (demonetises content)
   - Keep horror atmospheric, not gory

4. **Content that gets shared = more impressions = higher effective CPM**

5. **Watch time matters:** Even at 21s, if 80%+ watch fully, CPM stays high

## 📊 New Account Ramp-Up Strategy

**Week 1-2:** 1 video/day, all horror stories
**Week 3-4:** 1 video/day, start testing different story types
**Month 2+:** Up to 2 videos/day once account is established

TikTok gives new accounts a "boost window" — first 5-10 videos get extra distribution. Make them count.
