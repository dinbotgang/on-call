# On Call 📲

> Uber meets Hinge — for any contract work.

**On Call** is a two-sided marketplace connecting people who need skilled help *right now* with vetted local workers available to take the job. From snow removal to bartending, ski instructors to handymen — if it's a gig, it's on On Call.

## Quick Summary

- **Hire mode** — browse nearby workers, book instantly
- **Work mode** — toggle availability, accept jobs, get paid
- **Discovery UX** — Hinge-style rich profiles + swipe or browse
- **Immediacy** — on-demand first, scheduled second
- **Categories** — snow removal, bartending, caddying, driving lessons, handyman, car wash, and more
