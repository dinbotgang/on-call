### Root Causes Found
1. **Buy at bid = slow fills**: Limit order AT bid means tied for best bid, sellers go to whoever crossed first. 24% fill rate confirms this.
2. **2-tick drift threshold too sensitive**: 9 cancels from normal bid noise (3-8 tick moves), bot kept chasing prices upward without filling
3. **Sell targets stale after price moves**: 4 sell orders stuck 30-43% above current market â€” BTC $75k YES at $0.06 while price dropped to $0.041
4. **Balance crunch**: $4.27 balance, trades requiring $4.29-$4.70 all getting skipped instead of scaling down

### Fixes Applied (07:19 UTC restart)
1. **bid + 1 tick entry** â€” `buyAt = bid + tickSize` in `toSpreadOpp()`. Best bid in queue = seller hits us first. Costs ~1 tick ROI but dramatically improves fill probability.
2. **Drift threshold: 5 ticks + 5-min age** â€” was 2 ticks, instant. Now needs 5+ tick movement AND order must be 5+ minutes old before drift-cancel fires. Prevents chasing noise.
3. **Scale down to balance** â€” `actualBudget = min(usdcBudget, curBal * 0.95)` instead of skipping. Lets bot trade with whatever is available above $1 min.
4. **Stale sell detection** â€” stale monitor now fetches positions API, cancels any SELL order priced >30% above current market. Fill monitor immediately re-prices at curPrice+tick. Freed 4 stuck sells on first run.
