  • Referral campaigns through existing player networks
  • In-person at soccer complexes & training centers
  • Partner with private trainers for player referrals
  • Flyer campaigns at major soccer facilities
  • Content creation — field clips, podcast clips, social

Phase 2 — Club Partnerships (Month 6–12)

  • Target: 50+ clients before approaching clubs
  • Approach major DFW clubs (Solar, Texans, Athletico)
  • Option A: Club mandates service for youth teams — club earns ~25% of revenue
  • Option B: Club endorses & recommends via monthly emails
  • Work alongside team coaches for alignment

The Club Partnership Model

At scale, we integrate directly with youth clubs. The club earns ~25% of revenue — aligning their incentives with ours. We get guaranteed volume. They get a premium development service for their players. Win-win.

Future Scale Drivers
