FAQ

Common Questions

Who are the mentors?

+
Our mentors have played at the D1 NCAA and professional levels. We intentionally recruit mentors with elite playing backgrounds so that every athlete is learning from someone who has genuinely been where they want to go.

What age groups does this work for?

+
Our program is designed for competitive youth and high school soccer players, typically ages 13–18, who are serious about improving and exploring college opportunities. We also work with college-bound athletes.

What does the 36-hour response guarantee mean?

+
When you text your mentor, you're guaranteed a response within 36 hours. In practice, most messages are answered much faster — but we want you to know we're committed and accountable.

How does film analysis work?

+
Film analysis is an add-on service. You submit game or training footage, and your mentor provides a detailed breakdown — technical feedback, tactical observations, and specific areas to focus on. Pricing is discussed on inquiry.

Is this just for boys or girls too?

+
We welcome all athletes. We are actively building out a dedicated girls program with female mentors to ensure every player has a mentor who truly understands their experience.

What if I want to cancel?

+
There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime. We'd rather earn your loyalty through great service than lock you into something that isn't working.

How does academic support work?

+
Academic support is an optional add-on, available upon request. We connect athletes with advisors affiliated with top-10 institutions for guidance on college apps, academics, and balancing the demands of elite sport and school.

Do parents get updates?

+
Yes. We provide monthly updates to parents covering their athlete's progress, goals, and next steps. We believe families are part of the team and should stay informed.
