07 — Coach Alignment
Working With Your Coaches
We Work Alongside
Your Staff — Not
Around Them.

Elevate is designed to complement your coaching staff, not compete with them. We're not on the field — we're in the player's corner off it. And when clubs want us to, we align directly with what the coaching staff is building.

  • Mentors can be briefed on team goals and individual player needs
  • Player development plans can reflect in-season priorities set by your coaches
  • We reinforce — not replace — your coaching philosophy
  • Coaches can flag areas they want mentors to emphasize
  • Parents receive updates that are aligned with the club's messaging

During the season, we focus on mindset, film, and in-game development. Off-season is when strength, nutrition, and recruiting strategy take center stage. We adapt to where your players are.
